Why Copilot + Copilot Studio?

Two products, one AI strategy — designed to meet your teams where they work and scale with your business.

Why Microsoft Copilot

Microsoft Copilot is designed to help you draft, summarize, analyze, and accelerate workflows — right inside the Microsoft 365 apps your teams already use. It can help reduce repetitive tasks and free up time for higher-value work.

Why Copilot Studio

Copilot Studio helps you build custom agents tailored to your business processes. With low-code orchestration, enterprise connectors, and built-in governance, teams can often deploy agents in days rather than months.

Better Together

When you combine Copilot with Copilot Studio, you can extend built-in AI capabilities, ground responses in your own data, and customize agent behavior — all while maintaining enterprise-grade security and compliance controls.
